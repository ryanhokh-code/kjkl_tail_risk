The KJ Lambda measure captures the power-law exponent of the cross-sectional left tail. Higher values imply a fatter left tail (i.e. more extreme firm-specific crashes happening simultaneously).
The KL MEES measure tracks the systematic expected shortfall beyond a 10% VaR threshold. Elevated levels signify concentrated systemic risk exposure.
⚠️ Downside Implication for Europe (Euro Stoxx 50): The backtest shows significant predictive power for future returns. Elevated tail risk signals in this region are strongly correlated with sharp directional market corrections.
